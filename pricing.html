Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Starter plan?

The Starter plan includes basic AI integration for one use case, along with essential support and training. Perfect for businesses looking to implement AI in a specific area of their operations.

How does the annual billing discount work?

When you choose annual billing, you'll receive a 20% discount on your subscription. The amount is billed upfront for the entire year, providing significant savings.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remainder of your billing period. Our team will ensure a smooth transition.

What kind of support is included?

Each plan includes different levels of support:
• Starter: 8/5 email and chat support
• Professional: 16/7 priority support with 4-hour response time
• Enterprise: 24/7 premium support with dedicated account manager

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, our Enterprise plan is fully customizable to your needs. We'll work with you to create a tailored solution that meets your specific requirements and scale.

What's the implementation process like?

We follow a structured implementation process:
1. Initial consultation and needs assessment
2. Solution design and customization
3. Implementation and integration
4. Team training and onboarding
5. Ongoing support and optimization
